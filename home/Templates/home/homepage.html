About Docko

Docko is an online doctor appointment system designed to simplify the process of finding and scheduling appointments with doctors. Our platform connects patients with a wide range of medical professionals across various specializations, making it easy to find the right doctor for your needs.

Our Services

  • Find doctors based on specialization, location, and availability
  • Book appointments online
  • Manage appointments and reschedule if necessary
  • Access medical records and history securely
  • Chat with our AI-powered chatbot for quick assistance

Get Started

To begin using Docko, register as a patient or log in to your existing account. Once logged in, you can search for doctors, view their profiles, and book appointments online. Our analytics section provides insightful information on the distribution of doctors across various specialties, while our chatbot is available for quick support and assistance.
